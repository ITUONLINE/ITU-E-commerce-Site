[ Privacy Policy ]

Your privacy matters to us.

We want to be clear about how we collect and use information when you visit our website. This policy explains what we gather, why we gather it, and how we protect it.

Heads up: This policy may be updated from time to time. We recommend checking back periodically to stay informed. "We," "us," "our," and "ITU" refer collectively to ITU Online Training, LLC, its subsidiaries, and affiliates.

[ Data Collection ]

What information we collect.

We collect two types of information: personal information you provide directly to us, and general data we gather automatically when you use our website.

Registration forms.

Name, email, address, phone number, and account credentials when you sign up.

Account activity.

Pages visited, courses viewed, transactions, and how you interact with our platform.

Communications.

Any messages, emails, or inquiries you send to us directly.

Browser & visit data.

Browser type, domain, time on site, and referring pages — collected automatically.

We may also use cookies — small files placed on your device — to personalize your experience and verify access to certain content. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the site may not function as intended if you do.

[ Data Usage ]

How we use your information.

We use the information we collect to run and improve our services, not to sell your data. Specifically, we use it to:

  • ✓Verify your identity and manage your account
  • ✓Process transactions and send related updates
  • ✓Provide customer support and respond to your questions
  • ✓Improve the website experience and personalize content
  • ✓Send you relevant updates, notices, and offers from ITU
  • ✓Maintain the security and integrity of our systems
  • ✓Comply with applicable laws and legal obligations
[ Data Sharing ]

Who we share information with.

We may work with trusted third-party companies to help us deliver our services. These partners are permitted to use your information only as needed to perform those specific functions — nothing more.

We do not sell or share your personal information with unrelated businesses. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect the integrity of our platform, or to cooperate with a legal investigation.

[ Children's Privacy ]

Children's privacy.

Our website is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently done so, we will delete that information promptly.

[ Security ]

How we protect your data.

We use commercial firewall technology, SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protection, and 128-bit encryption to keep your information secure. Account credentials are never disclosed to any party without your authorization.
