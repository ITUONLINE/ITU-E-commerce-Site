[ Quick Answers ]

A few frequently asked questions.

After signing up, when can I access my training?

You will have immediate access to our LMS once you have completed the checkout process.

What do I get with my training plan?

Every plan includes full access to our complete course library — 3,000+ hours of on-demand video training, 22,000+ interactive practice questions, AI Study Buddy, certificates of completion, and career path programs. The only difference between plans is the billing period.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Monthly and quarterly plans can be canceled anytime before your next billing cycle. Annual plans can be canceled before renewal. Lifetime plans are a one-time purchase with no recurring charges. There are no cancellation fees on any plan.

What's the difference between monthly, quarterly, and annual?

All plans include the same content and features. The only difference is how you pay. Annual saves the most per month. The 90-Day Exam Cram is ideal for focused certification prep. Monthly gives you maximum flexibility with no commitment.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — you can take a quick test drive of our platform before committing. Create a free demo account to explore the LMS, browse courses, and see the learning experience firsthand.

Can I switch or upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from monthly to annual or lifetime at any time. Contact our support team and we'll help you transition to the plan that fits best.

Do I get a certificate when I complete a course?

Yes. Every course you complete comes with a verifiable certificate of completion that you can download, share on LinkedIn, or provide to your employer as proof of professional development.

Do I get access to new and updated courses?

Yes. With our Lifetime program, you get updated content for life. With all other plans, you get new and updated content for as long as your plan is active.
