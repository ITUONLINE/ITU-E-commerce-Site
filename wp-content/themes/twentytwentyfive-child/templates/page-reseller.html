[ Quick Answers ]

Common questions.

How does the white-label LMS work?

You get a fully branded learning management system with your logo, colors, and domain name. Your customers access training through your platform — they never see the ITU brand.

What courses are included?

Our catalog includes hundreds of IT training courses covering CompTIA, Cisco, AWS, Microsoft, cybersecurity, networking, and more. New courses are added regularly at no extra cost.

Is there a minimum purchase requirement?

We offer flexible pricing tiers to fit businesses of all sizes. Contact our partnership team to discuss the plan that works best for your organization.

How long does setup take?

Most partners are up and running within days. Our onboarding team handles technical integration and walks you through every step of the process.

Can I choose which courses to offer?

Absolutely. You have full control over which courses appear on your platform. Curate the catalog to match your audience and market focus.
